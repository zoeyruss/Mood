Mood is a community and social campaign which aims to question and inform others on the realities of those living with anxiety and depression.

About –

The ultimate goal of Mood is to get others to question societal outlook on hiding/mask mental illness. Mood visualizes mental illness throughmotion graphics, augmented reality, and every object, such as clothing, to visualizing anxiety and depression.

Having a visual representation of these disorders aims to help people who don't suffer to understand and to connect those who do suffer as a reminder they are not alone.
