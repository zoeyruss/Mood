anxiety

Anxiety is an emotion characterized by feelings of tension, worried thoughts and physical changes like increased blood pressure. People with anxiety disorders usually have recurring intrusive thoughts or concerns. They may avoid certain situations out of worry. They may also have physical symptoms such as sweating, trembling, dizziness or a rapid heartbeat.
- American Psychological Association

02.

feelings

How can you understand something you've never experienced? As a response to the two questions; "What is one thing you do you not like hearing when you're feeling anxious or depressed" and "Having Anxiety/Depression Is actually like...." visual responses aim to express outward appearances vs. inner struggle. The motion graphics visualize anxiety and depression through the lens of someone suffering while the text expresses societal outlooks.

03.

behaviors

What if mental health wasn’t seen as a flaw we need hide? Many people suffering from anxiety hide how they feel, leading to a mask from society. Using augmented reality technology these objects and clothing conceptize anxiety and depression through visually expressing how it feels to wear your emotions.
